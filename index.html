Intro

Perfection is not attainable, but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence -Vince Lombardi

In life we should always strive to not only do better but also be better. The challenge we give ourselves each day is to be better than we were yesterday. This is acheived by learning something new each day and not being afraid to make mistakes. This is because through making mistakes is how we learn and grow as humans. In my work you will not find perfection but you will find excellence and a strive to present the best work possible, which in turn allows me to bring a clients vision to life.

Read more about my journey here.

About

All my life I have loved to create and to find solutions for complex problems. When faced with challenges or opposition I take that resistance and use it as a driving force to push me to the thing I want to learn, or the goal I want to achieve. During high school I began learning web development and fell in love with it. Shortly after I was connected with a mentor who would later be the catalyst for me into the tech world.

A mentor connected me with other individuals like myself going down the path of self teaching and helped cultivate in each of us a hunger to learn more, which led me to Resilient Coders where I continued my journey and learned to make full stack web applications. Now I am wanting to help others bring their dreams to fruition and take their business to the next level. No matter how big or small the project, I want to work alongside you to help cultivate your ideas and make your vision come to life.
